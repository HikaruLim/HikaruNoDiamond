Alim 2016.04.23
---

Chinese ver:
打开“设备管理器”->监视器，
可以看到“通用即插即用监视器”被禁止导致该问题。
将其启用即可。

English ver:
open "device manager"->monitor,
you can find that the "universal plug and play monitor" was forbidden,which cause this problem.
so,just enable it.
